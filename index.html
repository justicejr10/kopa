Executive Summary

Executive Summary

Year in year out, corps members are churned out of the NYSC scheme to start their lives, many without any plans as to how to navigate the world. KOPA KONNECT will promote employability, training, job opportunities (job fair) or entrepreneurial corps members training on how to run a viable business and connect investment ready businesses to potential investors. It will also give corps members the opportunity to visit different places of attraction in Ogun state during their stay which will promote the culture and tourism of the state and Nigeria in general. It will also give corps members the opportunity to connect and bond with their colleagues at informal settings via hangouts, meetups and promote talents.

Value Proposition

A recent statistical survey revealed that no fewer than 30,000 graduates are mobilized annually for the NYSC Programme. This automatically means that a large number of persons are thrown into the employability sector to start their lives after 12 months of service. This project will not only prepare them to be employable or manage their businesses efficiently but also connect them to potential employers and investors respectively. It is well important to understand that “all work and no play makes jack a dull boy”, therefore to build synergy we’ll be organizing tours and hangouts for these corps members to unwind and cultivate potential relationships amongst themselves.

Business Model
